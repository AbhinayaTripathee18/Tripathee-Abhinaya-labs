A Dream That Started on Earth

My name is Abhinaya Tripathee. I am a student from Nepal with a lifelong passion for astronomy, astrophysics, physics, artificial intelligence, robotics, mathematics, and space exploration.

My dream is to become an astrophysicist, gain experience in the global space industry, and eventually establish Abhinaya Tripathee (CoDex) as a company that develops revolutionary technologies for humanity's future in space.

I believe science is not only about discovering the universe but also about creating technologies that improve life on Earth and beyond.
